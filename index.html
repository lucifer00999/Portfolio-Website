Objective

Generate high-quality leads for electronics product inquiries at a scalable and cost-efficient level.

Strategy & Execution

  • Focused on high-intent keywords (buy + product-based searches).
  • Structured campaigns by product categories for better control.

The Maximize Clicks strategy worked best for this traffic-focused goal. Broad keyword targeting successfully scaled the reach, while continuous negative keyword optimization prevented the CPC from inflating.
